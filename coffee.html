Filter Coffee

Filter Coffee is a traditional South Indian coffee made with freshly ground coffee beans and hot water, brewed slowly through a metal filter to extract a rich, aromatic essence. It is usually mixed with hot milk and sugar to create a creamy, flavorful cup that invigorates the senses.

Known for its strong aroma and smooth taste, Filter Coffee is perfect for starting the day or enjoying a relaxing afternoon. Each sip brings a bold yet balanced flavor, making it a favorite for coffee enthusiasts who love authentic, traditional coffee experiences.
