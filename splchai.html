Special Chai

Special Chai is a rich and aromatic tea made with a perfect blend of spices such as cardamom, cloves, ginger, and cinnamon. This flavorful brew is prepared slowly to allow the spices to release their natural oils, giving the tea a creamy texture and a strong, irresistible fragrance.

Known for its soothing warmth and refreshing taste, Special Chai is often enjoyed during evenings or cold weather. Each sip gives a burst of spicy sweetness, making it a favorite for chai lovers who prefer bold and enhanced flavors.
